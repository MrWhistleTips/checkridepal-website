Checkride Pal runs on iPhone and iPad with iOS 17 or later, plus an Apple Watch companion app for solo requirements tracking. Your data syncs across all devices via iCloud.

All your checkride cards, task library, grade modifiers, and weight categories sync automatically via CloudKit. Just sign into iCloud on your devices and your data stays up to date.

The app uses the standard Army letter grade system: A (90-100), B (80-89), C (70-79), and N/Unsatisfactory (69 and below). Grade weights are fully configurable per card, and weight categories let you adjust scoring multipliers globally.
